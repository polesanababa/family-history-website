Furthest Back Person

Here is the information that we know about the family's Furthest Back Person

The furthest back person in the family is Wakumama and Bunyala Dorofu, however the

year that they were born and died is unknown. The person who knew this is

Grandmother Ruth. With estimation, these people could have been from the 1800s.

However if we count a person that she has met, then Mbinga is the furthest back person

that has been met.
